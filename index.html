    Logo Animation

    •This logo animation is a finished piece for a fictional brand that I created for a fictional bamboo linen brand. I implemented two main concepts of animation for the final animation.

    •I applied the ideas of "timing," exaggeration," and "staging" to the movement of the bamboo ring and panda as it enters and exits out of the frame to emphasize the the the icon and leave the viewer remembering the brand's logo.

    •"Squash and stretch" was applied to the reveal of the brand's name for additional emphasis.

    Electric VS. Gasoine vehicle Infographic

    • An original infographic design created to take a closer look at the carbon footprints of electric vs. gasoline powered vehicles to aid in coming to an informed decision on which type of vehicle factually has the lowest carbon emissions.

    • The design presents comparison of vehicle manufacturing, battery manufacturing and vehicle operation.

    • Design decisions were made based on clarity and the play between warm and cool colors that are generally recognized among environmental aspects.

    Poblanos Ad Design

    • Original happy hour ad for the ficticious Poblano’s Tex-mex restaurant located in Austin, TX.

    • For this project, I imagined a vintage yet modern restaurant and created a poster design that conveyed the atmosphere I assigned to the establishment to promote its’ weekly happy hour.

    Poblanos Ad Design Variations

    • Shown here are two re-designs of the original ad in different formats.

    • The first is a banner style ad exploring the transformation of orientation.

    • The second re-design shifts the ad back to portrait orientation formatted ideally for social media usage.

    Cover Design - Tesla Model 3 Booklet

    • An original cover design for a digital booklet about the Tesla Model 3 electric car created for a class project.

    • The centered imagery emphasizes the specific model, while the juxtaposed text creates a sense of hierarchy and harmony between elements.
